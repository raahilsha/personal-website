About Me

Meet Raahil

Raahil is a co-founder of Zeta Surgical, a startup that uses augmented reality in-surgery to make operations safer, faster, and more accurate. He graduated from Harvard last year with a B.A. in Computer Science in the Mind, Brain, Behavior Program. Having done research on epigenomic factors affecting cancer at UT Dallas and on reconstructing the human connectome at Harvard, Raahil has extensive experience in computational biology and is interested in the intersection of technology and medicine. While not working, Raahil can be spotted roaming around Boston with a camera taking photographs of the city from rooftops and other tall places.
